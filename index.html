    In DIS, we offer quality education from Creche to Junior High School (JHS), in a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment designed to help every child thrive.

    At Divaid, our focus is on academic excellence, strong moral values, and holistic development. From early childhood through to JHS, we guide students with care, helping them build confidence, creativity, and critical thinking skills.

    We pride ourselves on maintaining high standards while keeping education affordable for families. We also believe in strong partnerships with parents, ensuring that every child receives the support they need both at school and at home.

    Enroll your child at Divaid International today — where learning is joyful, values are strong, and every child is prepared for a bright future.
